About the SRMIST Grading Architecture

This deployment maps the official structural framework of SRMIST academic regulations. SGPAs and CGPAs are derived through a weighted mathematical index calculation where total points accumulated across courses are scaled against the absolute sum of registered semester credits.

Conversion metric maps match internal university policies: $\text{Percentage} = \text{CGPA} \times 10$. Fail (F) and Absent (Ab) designations evaluate to an operational point weight of 0.
